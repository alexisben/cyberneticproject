--- title: CyberNeTic project (EN) layout: project text: >- In partnership with the **Gendarmerie Nationale**, the **CyberNeTic** project aims to conduct a research-action at the heart of information and communication sciences and criminology to study the forms of expression of violence through new technologies, and particularly that of **cyberharassment**. The aim is to analyze the discourse of experiences (victims, investigators, witnesses, etc.) and exchanges of conversations (sms, social networks, emails, etc.), in order to identify the spiral phenomena, the **strategies of influence and manipulation** initiated by the perpetrators. **The objectives** * To develop an emerging research dynamic based on a collection of qualitative data never before used in scientific work in the human and social sciences in France. * To conduct a research-action with a strong social impact that aims to improve the professional practices of police investigators through a comprehensive approach to cyber-violence and to strengthen their means of intervention in the field in the fight against cyberharassment. * To open and consolidate a scientific dialogue between the University of Bordeaux Montaigne and the Gendarmerie Nationale in order to develop a renewed scientific dynamic in criminology and in Information and Communication Sciences. team: - image: src: /assets/images/uploads/1516488732291.jpeg title: Marlène Dulaurans job: Réserviste citoyenne de la GN & Maître de conférences en Sciences de l’Information et de la Communication text: Maître de conférences et réserviste citoyenne de gendarmerie, elle est membre d’un groupe de recherche sur les « incivilités numériques » (axe COS du laboratoire MICA).
